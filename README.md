# British Airways Reviews Dashboard (Tableau)

## Project Overview
This project analyzes **customer reviews of British Airways** using an interactive dashboard built in **Tableau**. The dashboard explores customer satisfaction across multiple service metrics and allows users to filter and analyze reviews by traveler characteristics, aircraft type, geography, and time.

The goal of this project is to identify patterns in passenger satisfaction and highlight areas where airline service performs well or needs improvement.

## Dataset
The dataset contains customer reviews of British Airways, including:

- Overall rating
- Cabin Staff Service rating
- Entertainment rating
- Food & Beverages rating
- Ground Service rating
- Seat Comfort rating
- Value for Money rating
- Traveler type
- Seat class
- Aircraft type
- Country of reviewer
- Date of review

The data covers reviews from **March 2016 to October 2023**.

---

## Dashboard Features

### KPI Summary
The top section displays the **average ratings across key service metrics**, including:

- Overall Rating
- Cabin Staff Service
- Entertainment
- Food & Beverages
- Ground Service
- Seat Comfort
- Value for Money

These KPIs provide a quick overview of customer satisfaction.

---

### Geographic Distribution
A **world map visualization** shows the **average overall rating by country**, allowing users to explore how satisfaction varies across different regions.

---

### Time Trend Analysis
A **monthly trend line** shows how the **average overall rating changes over time**, helping identify fluctuations in customer sentiment.

---

### Aircraft Performance
A horizontal bar chart analyzes:

- **Average overall rating by aircraft type**
- **Number of reviews per aircraft**

This helps compare aircraft models in terms of customer satisfaction and review frequency.

---

### Interactive Filters
Users can interact with the dashboard using filters to customize the analysis:

- Review metric (Overall rating, food, seat comfort, etc.)
- Month / time range
- Traveler type (Business, Leisure, Family, etc.)
- Seat class (Economy, Business, First)
- Aircraft group
- Continent

These filters allow deeper exploration of passenger experience.

## Key Insights

Some key observations from the dashboard:

- The **average overall rating is 4.2**, indicating moderate customer satisfaction.
- **Entertainment (1.4)** receives the lowest rating among service metrics.
- **Cabin staff service (3.3)** is one of the higher-rated aspects of the airline experience.
- Certain aircraft types receive noticeably higher ratings compared to others.
- Customer satisfaction fluctuates over time, suggesting possible operational or service changes.
